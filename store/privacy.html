Kitchenline

Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

Kitchenline doesn't require an account, doesn't run analytics or ad tracking, and doesn't build a profile of you. Your skill ratings, weak-shot picks, and progress live only on your device. Here's exactly what happens when you use the app.

What stays on your device

  • Your self-rated skill levels for each drill category.
  • Your selected weak-shot tags and today's practice-minutes setting.
  • Your daily progress tracker — the locked-in rep arcs shown on the Home tab, which reset automatically each day.
  • The most recently generated AI weekly practice plan, cached so you don't lose it between launches.

None of this is uploaded anywhere, backed up to a server we run, or shared with any third party. It lives in this app's local storage on your iPhone.

What Pro's AI features send, and why

Kitchenline Pro's two AI features — the weekly practice plan and ghost-rally mode — call a shared, stateless backend we operate (an "AI proxy") to reach a text-generation model:

  • Weekly plan: your selected weak-shot tags (e.g. "Third Shot Drop") and how many minutes you have to practice today.
  • Ghost-rally: the name and one-line summary of the specific bundled drill you're practicing.

No account identifier, name, email, location, or device identifier is ever included in these requests. The proxy does not log or persist request content — it forwards your prompt to a model provider, returns the response, and keeps nothing. If a request fails or the response can't be parsed, Kitchenline falls back to a plan or sequence generated entirely on-device — no data leaves your phone in that case.

What Kitchenline never does

  • No account creation, no sign-in, no email collection.
  • No location access, no contacts access, no camera or microphone access.
  • No advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, no cross-app tracking.
  • No selling or sharing of any data, because none is collected that could be shared.

Subscriptions

Kitchenline Pro is an auto-renewable subscription billed through your Apple ID via StoreKit. Apple handles all payment information — Kitchenline never sees or stores your card details. You can manage or cancel your subscription any time in your device's Settings app.

Deleting your data

Settings → Erase All Data removes every skill rating, weak-shot pick, cached plan, and progress entry stored by the app immediately. Deleting the app does the same, since nothing exists outside it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change too. Material changes will be reflected in a new App Store version's release notes.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the developer via the App Store support link on Kitchenline's product page.
